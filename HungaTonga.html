    Hunga Tonga-Hunga Haʻapai Eruptions

    Back to other Disaters

    Hunga Tonga-Hunga Haʻapai has erupted three different times since 2009 and as a result the land area has changed drastically. As a result, we can watch the vegetation recover in satellite imagery. Although the vegetation is too small to make out using traditional methods, these false color composites depict the vegetation growth after the 2015 volcanic eruption. Using infrared 534 false color composites vegetation is displayed as red.
