    2019-2020 Australian Bushfires

    Back to other Disaters

    Lack of soil moisture and particularly dry conditions led to a premature start to the bushfire season in Australia’s Central Queensland in 2019. The fires burnt nearly 100,000 square miles and were responsible for 450 deaths, 33 directly from the fire.
